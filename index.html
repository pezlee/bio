I later led the implementation of the CS Ticketing System and introduced the Internet Call feature, significantly improving support accessibility and user satisfaction.

These experiences have shaped my commitment to building meaningful customer experiences through innovation and collaboration. I'm always open to connecting with professionals who share a passion for elevating service excellence.
